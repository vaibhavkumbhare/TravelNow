Select Eco-Friendly Accommodations
Stay in eco-certified hotels, lodges, or hostels that prioritize sustainability.
Choose accommodations that implement energy-efficient systems, such as solar panels and water-saving fixtures.
Look for lodgings that actively reduce waste through recycling, composting, and minimizing single-use plastics.
Support locally owned lodgings to benefit the local economy and reduce the carbon footprint of large hotel chains.
